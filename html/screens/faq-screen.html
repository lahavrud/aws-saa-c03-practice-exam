Platform & Usage

Is AWS Lahavda free to use?

Yes! AWS Lahavda is completely free to use. Simply sign in with your Google account to access all features, including practice exams, progress tracking, and detailed explanations.

How do I save my progress?

Your progress is automatically saved to Firebase when you sign in with Google. Your data is synced across all your devices, so you can study on your phone, tablet, or computer seamlessly.

What's the difference between Review Mode and Test Mode?

Review Mode: Get immediate feedback after each answer with detailed explanations. Perfect for learning and understanding concepts.

Test Mode: Simulates the real exam with a 130-minute timer. You'll see your results and explanations only after completing the entire test.

Can I practice by specific AWS domain?

Yes! Select "By Domain" from the main menu to practice questions from a specific domain (Design Secure Architectures, Design Resilient Architectures, etc.) across all available tests.

How do I mark questions for review?

Click the "Mark" button while viewing a question. Marked questions are highlighted in the question navigation bar, making it easy to review them later.

Can I export my progress data?

Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Data Management ‚Üí Export Data to download your progress as a JSON file. You can import it later or use it to sync across different browsers.

About the SAA-C03 Exam

What is the AWS SAA-C03 exam?

The AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate (SAA-C03) exam validates your ability to design and deploy scalable, highly available, and fault-tolerant systems on AWS. It's one of the most popular AWS certifications.

How long is the exam?

The exam is 130 minutes long and consists of 65 questions. That's approximately 2 minutes per question, so time management is crucial.

What score do I need to pass?

The passing score is 720 out of 1000 points (72%). However, AWS doesn't reveal the exact scoring methodology, so it's best to aim for consistent scores above 80% on practice exams.

What topics are covered in the exam?

The exam covers four domains:

  • Design Secure Architectures (30%): Security, IAM, encryption, compliance
  • Design Resilient Architectures (26%): Availability, backups, disaster recovery
  • Design High-Performing Architectures (24%): Performance, scaling, caching
  • Design Cost-Optimized Architectures (20%): Cost optimization, pricing models

How much does the exam cost?

The exam fee is $150 USD. You can schedule your exam through the AWS Certification portal or at a Pearson VUE test center.

How should I prepare for the exam?

We recommend:

  1. Get hands-on experience with AWS services
  2. Take multiple practice exams (aim for 80%+ consistently)
  3. Review AWS documentation and best practices
  4. Focus on weak domains using our domain-specific practice
  5. Understand the AWS Well-Architected Framework

Technical & Support

Do I need to create an account?

Yes, you need to sign in with your Google account to save your progress and access all features. This is free and only takes a few seconds.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use Firebase (Google's secure cloud platform) to store your data. Your progress is encrypted and only accessible through your Google account. We don't share your data with third parties.

Can I use this on mobile devices?

Absolutely! AWS Lahavda is fully responsive and works great on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Your progress syncs across all devices automatically.

What browsers are supported?

We support all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of your browser.

Can I reset my progress?

Yes, you can reset your progress from Settings ‚Üí Danger Zone ‚Üí Reset All Progress. Warning: This action cannot be undone and will permanently delete all your progress data.

Where do the practice questions come from?

Our practice questions come from multiple trusted sources including Stephane Maarek, Tutorials Dojo, and Sergey. These are widely recognized as high-quality practice materials for AWS certification exams.
